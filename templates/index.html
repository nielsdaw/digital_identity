Social Me

What?
This site provides a free service, where you can view some of the information you provide to social medias.

How?
Choose the social media profiles you want to connect to the service. The service will then retrieve your information from the chosen social media(s) and create a temporary Social Me.

Why?
The main purpose of the project is to develop a service available for everyone, to create a simple overview of which information we put on different platforms.

What happens to my information?
None of your information from social medias is stored. The information you provide is used to create a temporary 'Social Me', which only you can see
